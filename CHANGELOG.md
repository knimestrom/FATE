## [0.1.0] - 2026-04-29

### Added

- Initial Fate Market MVP protocol package.
- Event signal ingestion and event candidate discovery.
- AI market intelligence generation for YES and NO prediction markets.
- Binary pricing pool for outcome share trading.
- Collateral ledger, position accounting, trade history, and fee tracking.
- Buy-side and sell-side trading lifecycle.
- Portfolio valuation for open market exposure.
- Close-time locking, evidence settlement, and redemption flows.
- Solana instruction builders for open, buy, sell, resolve, and redeem actions.
- Bundled Fate Market logo and favicon assets.
- README coverage for architecture, modules, configuration, usage, roadmap, FAQ, and brand assets.
- Deterministic protocol tests and TypeScript build output.
